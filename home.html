STEP BY STEP ROADMAP TO COMPLETE INTERNSHIP

  • Register For An Internship STEP 1
    Apply For An Internship In Your Interested Domain.Select The Internship Duration according to your college Academic. Students From Any Branch And At Any Level. Can Apply For The Internship
  • Get Login Access and Offer Letter STEP 2
    Our Team Will Review Your Application, Send You An Offer Letter and Login Access Via mail within 5-7 Working days For The Internship.
  • Get Internship Batch STEP 3
    After Receiving The Offer Letter, You Will Be Assigned A Batch For A Specific Month During Which You Will Work On The Internship.
  • Project Allocation STEP 4
    You Will Be Assigned Some Projects on Dashboard That Are Beneficial For Enhancing Your Skills In Your Interested Domain.
  • Learn, Execute, Complete & Submit STEP 5
    Complete The Projects Allocated To You With in The Given time And After Completing Your Projects, Submit Them On The Internship Portal For Evaluation.
  • Project Submission Verification STEP 6
    The Slash Mark Team Will Verify Your Project Submissions Based On Their Completion Criteria.
  • Get Internship Completion Certificate STEP 7
    After Successfully Evaluating Your Projects, You Will Receive An Internship Completion Certificate.

Frequently Asked Questions

To be eligible for the internship, you must be undergoing a degree in B.E / B. Tech / BBA / MBA or any other degree. Additionally, you should possess knowledge of software and coding languages.
The internship duration ranges from 4 weeks to 16 weeks.
Currently, the internship is unpaid. However, there may be opportunities for paid internships in the future.
You can apply for the internship program either via the AICTE portal or directly from our website. Click on "Apply Now" to access the registration form, and wait for the approval of your Internship Offer Letter.
The internship program is project-based and consists of three levels of projects: 3 Basic Projects, 2 Mini Intermediate Projects, and 1 Major Advanced Project. There are no training or live classes; instead, you will gain practical experience by working on real projects with specific deadlines.
Upon successful completion of the internship, you will be awarded 3 credits. These credits are approved by AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education), adding value to your resume and career prospects.
Interns have the opportunity to receive rewards such as company T-shirt, cap, or premium bag. These rewards are given to top-tiered students based on their performance and contributions during the internship.
